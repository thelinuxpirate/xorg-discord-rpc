* Build Setup
- Download [[https://dl-game-sdk.discordapp.net/2.5.6/discord_game_sdk.zip][Discord-Game-SDK (Legacy: v2.5.6)]]
- Extract C++ Files:

#+begin_src
discord-sdk
├── include
│   ├── achievement_manager.h
│   ├── activity_manager.h
│   ├── application_manager.h
│   ├── core.h
│   ├── discord.h
│   ├── event.h
│   ├── ffi.h
│   ├── image_manager.h
│   ├── lobby_manager.h
│   ├── network_manager.h
│   ├── overlay_manager.h
│   ├── relationship_manager.h
│   ├── storage_manager.h
│   ├── store_manager.h
│   ├── types.h
│   ├── user_manager.h
│   └── voice_manager.h
├── lib
│   └── libdiscord_game_sdk.so
└── src
    ├── achievement_manager.cpp
    ├── activity_manager.cpp
    ├── application_manager.cpp
    ├── core.cpp
    ├── image_manager.cpp
    ├── lobby_manager.cpp
    ├── network_manager.cpp
    ├── overlay_manager.cpp
    ├── relationship_manager.cpp
    ├── storage_manager.cpp
    ├── store_manager.cpp
    ├── types.cpp
    ├── user_manager.cpp
    └── voice_manager.cpp
#+end_src

Now your project directory should look something like:
#+begin_src
├── build
├── discord-sdk
│   ├── include
│   ├── lib
│   └── src
├── include
└── src
#+end_src
Once you get to this point just run the ~compile.sh~ & ~run.sh~ files & youre good to go
